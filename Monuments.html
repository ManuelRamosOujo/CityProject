Torre del Oro

It's structured in three different sections, the first, and biggest, one was built around 1220 by Abu I-Ula's commands. The second part was built in the XIV century by king Pedro I. The last part of this monument, which is the dome, was built in 1760 by a Engineer called Sebastian Van der Borcht.
